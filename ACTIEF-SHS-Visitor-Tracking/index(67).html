Visitor Rules

- Visitors must register before entering.
- Follow school policies at all times.
- Present identification if requested.
- Respect students and staff.
- Restricted areas are prohibited without permission.

Privacy and Safety

Visitor information such as name, student ID, and purpose of visit is collected to ensure campus security and proper monitoring of visitors.

The information is used strictly within school premises for documentation and safety purposes only. No data will be shared outside the institution.

All records are stored locally and accessed only by authorized administrators.
